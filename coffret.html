coffret D'artist: Marc Riboud

it's an editorial and packaging design to represent the artist of my choice's work and its connection to my graphic world. i did an extensive research on the artist's work and life, and created a visual identity that reflects the Riboud's work and my own style without touching the original work. the box includes a 324 pages book (A4), posters(A2, A5), film contaiers(portraits),keychains and a tiny book. the poster is a visual representation of the artist's work, and the tiny book is more of a personal touch on his work.
